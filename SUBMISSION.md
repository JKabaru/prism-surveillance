- **Project Title:** PRISM: Predictive Risk & Instability Surveillance Module
- **Short Description:** An AI-powered forensic intelligence platform for detecting coordinated fraud rings and proactive regime shifts in affiliate networks.
- **Long Description:** 
  PRISM (Predictive Risk & Instability Surveillance Module) is a next-generation surveillance suite designed for high-frequency financial and affiliate networks. It addresses the growing challenge of coordinated fraud by combining temporal correlation engines with proactive behavioral monitoring.

  Key innovations include:
  1. **Temporal Correlation:** Detecting "Mirror Trading" rings by clustering sub-second trade synchronization.
  2. **Regime Monitoring:** Identifying "Sleeper Agents" through Z-score baseline deviation analysis before they exit the ecosystem.
  3. **Glass-Box Reasoning:** A transparent agentic pipeline that provides a full audit trail of the AI's forensic traversal.
  4. **Investigation Workbench:** Interactive Nexus Graphs and automated evidence synth briefs (HTML/PDF) for compliance teams.
